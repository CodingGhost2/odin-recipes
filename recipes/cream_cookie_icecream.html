Cookies and Cream Icecream Recipe


Description

It summer time and the heat is razing through your home so you're looking for something to cool you down. What better than eating a sugary refreshing pint of ice cream? Here how you can make your own Ice Cream at home for much more cheaper and also allowing you to implement this method with any flavor you want!


This recipes is for oreo cookie and cream icecream however you can apply this with any other flavor you would like

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Oreos
  • Condensed Milk
  • Vanilla Extract


Steps

  1. Whip 2 cups of heavy cream until stiff
  2. Add in the Condensed Milk and Mix
  3. Crunch the oreos into piece and mix into the mixture
  4. add in 1 tps of Vanilla Extract and Mix
  5. put into a container and refridgeate
  6. Enjoy!
